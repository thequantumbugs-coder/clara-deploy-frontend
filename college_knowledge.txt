PART 1: INSTITUTION OVERVIEW
ABOUT SVIT
Sai Vidya Institute of Technology, established in 2008 by the Sri Sai Vidya Vikas Shikshana Samithi and founded by a team of eminent academicians, SVIT is a premier institution affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and recognized by the Government of Karnataka. The institute is committed to providing a dynamic learning environment through its highly qualified, dedicated teaching and non-teaching staff, emphasizing academic rigor, innovation, and holistic development to ensure student success in a competitive global landscape. SVIT offers a vibrant atmosphere featuring modern infrastructure, well-equipped libraries, and advanced laboratories, where students benefit from active clubs, cultural activities, and continuous mentoring from experienced faculty. Furthermore, the institution maintains strong industry collaborations to facilitate internships and hands-on training, supported by a dedicated placement cell that ensures robust career opportunities with leading recruiters.

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
Computer Science & Engineering is a fast evolving branch of Engineering with rapid advancements in the areas like Cloud Computing, Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Distributed Systems and so on., creating a huge requirement of software engineers across the globe. Computer Science & Engineering deals with the theoretical foundations of information and computation along with practical techniques for implementation and application. Computer Science engineers can contribute as Software Developers, Hardware Engineers, Database Analysts (SQL), AI and Data Science Engineers, System analysts or System Engineers, Security analysts, Business System Database Developer, Data Modeller, Software Quality Assurance (QA) apart from multi-disciplinary research.

• Intake: 180 students | Duration: 4 Years
• Focus: Cloud Computing, Software Development, AI, Deep Learning, Distributed Systems
• Career Paths: Software Developers, Hardware Engineers, Database Analysts, AI/Data Science Engineers, System Analysts, Security Analysts
• Key Strengths: Modern labs, experienced faculty, industry collaborations, internships
